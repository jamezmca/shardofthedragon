i want a tiny map at 0.5 opacity in the top right corner that shows the full context, and where we are, and what has been mowed.

we play as a little red mower, and i want the gress to be a little textured.

the game should be in the same style as the original pokemon games (but we play as the little red mower). it's called mowing madness. i want the top scores (in XXMYYS to be showing along the top as a banner that scrolls through my top 5 results).

moving moves at a constant speed, and forward/backward speed adjustments are instant. left and right turn radius are pretty responsive (so a sharp turn radius).
